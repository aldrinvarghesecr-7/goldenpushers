# Team Member Images

Place your team member photos in this folder.

## Naming Convention

Use the following filenames (matching the order in `app/about/page.tsx`):

| File | Team Member |
|---|---|
| `member-1.jpg` | 1st team member |
| `member-2.jpg` | 2nd team member |
| `member-3.jpg` | 3rd team member |
| `member-4.jpg` | 4th team member |
| `member-5.jpg` | 5th team member |
| `member-6.jpg` | 6th team member |

## Recommendations

- **Aspect ratio**: 3:4 (portrait) works best
- **Resolution**: At least 800×1067px
- **Format**: `.jpg` or `.webp`
- **Style**: Consistent lighting and background for a cohesive look
